Must-Visit Places

Iconic Attractions
of Sri Lanka

From ancient rock fortresses to misty highlands and sun-drenched beaches, Sri Lanka offers a world of wonder in a single island.

01

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

The legendary 5th-century sky palace rising 200m above the jungle plains — a UNESCO World Heritage Site and an absolute must.

UNESCO
02

Temple of the Sacred Tooth — Kandy

Sri Lanka's holiest Buddhist temple, home to the sacred relic of the Buddha, set in the heart of the lush hill capital.

Spiritual
03

Yala National Park

One of the world's best leopard-spotting destinations, also home to elephants, sloth bears and hundreds of bird species.

Wildlife
04

Ella & Nine Arch Bridge

Postcard-perfect highlands with famous train rides, hiking trails up Little Adam's Peak and the iconic colonial-era viaduct.

Highlands
05

Mirissa Beach

A crescent of golden sand on the south coast, famed for whale watching (December–April) and vibrant sunset scenes.

Coastal
06

Anuradhapura — Ancient Capital

One of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities, with towering white stupas and sacred Bodhi trees spanning 2,500 years.

UNESCO
07

Galle Fort

A beautifully preserved Dutch colonial fort city with cobblestone lanes, boutique cafes and sweeping ocean views.

Colonial
08

Adam's Peak (Sri Pada)

A sacred pilgrimage mountain climbed by all faiths, offering a breathtaking cone shadow sunrise at the summit.

Pilgrimage
